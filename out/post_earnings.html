Daily Brief Earnings NVDA Pre-call
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Ahead of NVIDIA's 1/8 earnings call, the market is not debating a single quarter but the durability of a premium valuation. NVDA trades at 52.3x P/E versus the AI semiconductor peer median of 41.95x, a 25% premium supported by 70.1% gross margin and platform stickiness.

Using the TTM EPS anchor of $3.61, the valuation map is straightforward: peer-median 41.95x implies about $151, the current 52.3x multiple maps to roughly $189, and the $262.79 target implies an estimated 72.8x.

The CES 2026 Blackwell Ultra reveal (2.5x inference performance, +40% efficiency, AWS/Azure/GCP launch partners) shifted attention to inference economics. On the call, watch delivery pacing, pricing discipline, and whether cloud deployments are being pulled forward. Any ambiguity on supply visibility or pricing tone can compress multiples quickly; clear confirmation can extend the premium and justify staying in the higher band. If delivery signals slip, the re-rating toward the 41.95x peer median becomes the most likely reset.

一句話結論

CES 提振推理敘事，法說成為溢價是否延續的驗證點。52.3x 對 41.95x 的溢價能否守住，關鍵在交付節奏與定價權語氣。

市場在聽什麼

CES 發布後，市場在交易的不是「新品規格」，而是：

  • 推理成本曲線下移速度：2.5x 效能 + FP4 支援能否讓推理規模化
  • 雲端部署節奏：AWS/Azure/GCP 首發合作是宣示還是有時間表
  • 供給可得性：新品再強，交付跟不上 = 估值先修正
  • 定價權語氣：高溢價能否靠毛利率與價格紀律維持

1/8 法說會觀察重點

  1. 雲端客戶導入/部署節奏是否「前置」？
  2. 交付節奏與供給可得性：瓶頸緩解 or 持續？
  3. 推理相關產品定價：有無「定價權」變化訊號？
  4. FP4/推理優化：功能亮點 or 可規模化方向？
